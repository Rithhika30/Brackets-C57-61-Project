My Favourite Hobby
Playing Netball

Hi! My name is Rithhika. Today I am going to tell you about my hobby which is playing Netball!

I Like to play Netball because it's an indoor sport and outdoor, so you can play it any time of the year, any time of day and it's a great social event where you can have fun with friends who are beginners and also friends that really know how to play. My Favourite Player

Geva Mentor


Geva Kate Mentor, CBE (born 17 September 1984 in Bournemouth, England) is an English International netball player. Mentor was selected for the England national team in 2000, debuting the following year against New Zealand, at age 16. She currently plays for the Collingwood Magpies in the Australian Super Netball league, and prior to that was a two-time premiership captain at the Sunshine Coast Lightning.

My Inspiration

Renae Hallinan


Renae Hallinan,Australia/Adelaide Thunderbirds You have to understand netball to understand why Hallinan is such a great player. Hallinan is a dogged one-on-one defender who shuts down wing attacks, putting pressure on goal attacks to play two positions and leaving them unable to put up shots. Renae Hallinan (born 18 July 1986 in Melbourne, Australia) is an Australian international netballer. Primarily a wing-defence and centre player, Hallinan is a member of the Australian national team and plays in the ANZ Championship for the Adelaide Thunderbirds.
